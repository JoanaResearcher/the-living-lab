# The Living Lab: RFTA Validator

**Lead Technical Researcher: Joana Camp** *Senior, Old Dominion University | B.S. Occupational and Technical Studies*

## Professional Profile
[cite_start]I am an Occupational and Technical Studies Senior at Old Dominion University (3.95 GPA) and the Lead Technical Researcher for **The Living Lab**. My work is grounded in nearly **30 years of lived experience** navigating federal housing systems. [cite_start]In Fall 2025, I transitioned this expertise into a formal ethnographic research project to mitigate the "**Bandwidth Tax**"—the 30% drain on cognitive resources caused by administrative friction[cite: 601, 607].

Using AI-assisted coding, I developed the **RFTA Validator**, a diagnostic tool officially submitted to the **OpenAI Codex Creator Challenge** via Handshake. [cite_start]This tool replaces 20-page "Information Dumps" with bilingual "**Hard Stops**" to eliminate the "**Signature Gap**" and prevent program attrition[cite: 486, 611, 615].
